Frequently Asked Questions

What is pickatable?

pickatable is a restaurant discovery platform offering a 50% off on food on a table reservation for two, three or four people. The perfect solution for food lovers to explore something new.

How does it work?

Easy. Download the App and search for a restaurant you want to try out. Select one of the available slots of the early tables and reserve it for €8 . That's it!

What about the drinks?

Our deal is firmly 50% off on food only, drinks are always full price.

What if we are more than four people?

It's great to be together, and we're working on increasing the number of diners but for the time being, the maximum number of guests per table reservation is four, with only one table per group.

Adding extra tables unfortunately is not permitted.
But hey, stay tuned for more information!

How do restaurants benefit?

pickatable is a win-win solution for customers and restaurants alike. We encourage customers to be the first to reserve a table during a generally quiet period of the day.

What if I'm late?

Because no one likes latecomers, the deal is no longer valid if you arrive more than 15 minutes late. This keeps things fair for both diners and restaurants.

What is the policy over public holidays?

Restaurants are entitled to choose days and times for "table picking". Availability on public holidays is entirely on them and isn't therefore guaranteed, but there's always hope in the universe.
